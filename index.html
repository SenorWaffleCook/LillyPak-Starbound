Welcome to the LillyPak mod pack page.

This is a modpack created by thakyZ, mostly for private poruposes. But, you are more than welcome to use this for your self. None of the mods in the mod pack are included, do not have full verified permission to be here. If you are one of the mod owners, you can email tzcraftnsr@outlook.com to agree that you allow your mod/modpack to be added to this, or you can request to have thakyZ remove it. All mods except the mod folder(s) /LillyMod/ are not owned by thakyZ. Though the mod(s) owned by thakyZ are licenced under the Apache Licence.
